Privacy Policy for FillTheBlank

FillTheBlank respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

FillTheBlank does not collect any personal data. All text you enter, generate, or edit within the app remains on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

2. Data Handling

FillTheBlank processes all content locally on your device. The app does not access external files or folders, and no data is transmitted outside your device.

3. In‑App Purchases

FillTheBlank offers an optional in‑app purchase to unlock additional features. All payments are processed securely by Google Play. The app does not receive or store any payment information.

4. Third‑Party Services

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third‑party SDKs.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect personal information from children or any users.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the latest version will be provided through the app or the store listing.
