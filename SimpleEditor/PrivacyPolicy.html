Privacy Policy for SimpleEditor SimpleEditor respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal information. 1. Information We Collect SimpleEditor does not collect any personal data. All text files you open or edit remain on your device and are never uploaded to any server. 2. The app cannot access any other files or folders on your device. No file content is transmitted outside your device. 3. Children’s Privacy The app does not collect personal information from children or any users. 6.
