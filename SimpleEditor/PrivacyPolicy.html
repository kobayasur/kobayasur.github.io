Privacy Policy for SimpleEditor

SimpleEditor respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

SimpleEditor does not collect any personal data.
All text files you open or edit remain on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

2. File Access

The app uses the Android Storage Access Framework (SAF) to open and save files. This means:

  • The app can access only the files you explicitly select.
  • The app cannot access any other files or folders on your device.
  • No file content is transmitted outside your device.

3. In‑App Purchases

SimpleEditor offers an optional in‑app purchase to unlock additional features.
All payments are processed securely by Google Play.
The app does not receive or store any payment information.

4. Third‑Party Services

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third‑party SDKs.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect personal information from children or any users.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the latest version will be provided through the app or the store listing.
